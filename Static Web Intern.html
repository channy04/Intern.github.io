Is the Lunchkit Club a contract?

No, you are free to come and go as you please. We don't believe in locking you into any long-term contracts. When you purchase a monthly pre-paid membership, you have 1 week to cancel for a full refund.


Is there a discount If i refer a friend?

Yes, we offer referral discounts for members on Launchfit Club memberships. Each friend you refer who takes up a monthly membership entitles you to 1 weeks free club membership.


Do you run female only workshops?

No - We foster an inclusive atmosphere where all ages, genders and beliefs are accepted equally. Training with a diverse group leads to a more holistic fitness experience for all.
